This is the first post on my new site. I built it to keep my projects, games,
reading, and the occasional write-up in one place while I apply for
quantitative trading and research roles.

## What you'll find here

- **Projects** — tools and experiments I've built, with links to source.
- **Games** — small browser games, playable right here.
- **Reading** — books I've read, with ratings and short notes.
- **Blog** — notes on markets, code, probability, and whatever else I'm chewing on.

I expect to write mostly short posts: a neat result, a bug that taught me
something, or a question I'm thinking through. More soon.
